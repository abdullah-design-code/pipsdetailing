Understanding Ceramic Coating Technology

Discover why ceramic coatings provide superior protection compared to traditional wax

The Problem with Traditional Wax

Automotive paint is surprisingly soft (1-4 on the Mohs scale) and porous, making it vulnerable to scratches, UV damage, and environmental contaminants.

Traditional wax simply sits on top of your paint and washes away quickly, providing only temporary protection.

How Ceramic Coatings Work

Ceramic coatings use advanced silica technology that chemically bonds with your vehicle's clear coat. Think of it like epoxy resin sealing wood - it fills the pores, smooths the surface, and becomes significantly tougher.

This creates a glass-like finish that's more durable and long-lasting than any wax or polymer sealant.
